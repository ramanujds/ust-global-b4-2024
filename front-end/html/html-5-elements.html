Section

Section is a thematic grouping of content, typically with a heading.

Article

Article is a self-contained composition in a document, page, application, or site, which is intended to be independently distributable or reusable.

Aside

Aside is a portion of a document whose content is only indirectly related to the document's main content.

Footer

Footer is a footer for its nearest ancestor sectioning content or sectioning root element. A footer typically contains information about its section such as who wrote it, links to related documents, copyright data, and the like.
